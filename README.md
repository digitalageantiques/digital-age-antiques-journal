# Digital Age Antiques Journal

**A long-term learning journal about early digital products, cameras, physical media, object design, cultural memory, and future collectibles in the AI era.**

这是一个关于数字时代旧物的长期学习记录。

这里关注的，不只是一个东西有多旧、值多少钱，也包括它曾经怎样被使用、为什么被淘汰、它代表了哪一段技术变化，以及它为什么可能在未来重新被人重视。

The journal may include observations about old cameras, early digital devices, music players, physical media, game systems, recording tools, communication devices, and other objects from the transition between the analog and digital worlds.

记录内容可以来自旧相机、早期数码产品、音乐播放器、实体媒介、游戏设备、录音工具、通信产品，以及其他处于模拟时代与数字时代交界处的真实物件。

## What This Journal Records

This journal records real observations, questions, comparisons, mistakes, discoveries, and changing opinions.

It may explore:

* early digital products
* film and digital cameras
* physical media
* object design
* technology history
* cultural memory
* provenance and originality
* condition and completeness
* scarcity and demand
* future collectibles
* real-world price comparison
* personal judgment

这个仓库不会只记录“买了什么”，还会记录为什么注意到它、第一次判断是什么、后来查到了什么、哪里判断错了，以及新的信息如何改变原来的看法。

## Journal Structure

```text
journal/
object-notes/
comparisons/
projects/
```

* `journal/` — learning notes and changing ideas
* `object-notes/` — observations about individual objects
* `comparisons/` — comparisons between products, brands, and periods
* `projects/` — longer research and practical projects

## Starting Principle

An object is not valuable only because it is old.

Its meaning may come from design, use, condition, cultural memory, technological importance, scarcity, provenance, and the way people understand it over time.

旧，不等于有价值。

真正值得长期观察的，是一件物品如何连接技术、生活、记忆、设计和人的真实使用经历。
