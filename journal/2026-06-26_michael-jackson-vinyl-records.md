# 2026-06-26 Michael Jackson Vinyl Records

Today I bought several Michael Jackson vinyl records, including *Forever, Michael*, *Music & Me*, *Off the Wall*, and one white label record. This is not a professional collection record yet. It is more like an early learning note. What caught my attention first was not only the name on the covers, but the feeling that these objects had already passed through time. The covers have wear, the plastic sleeves are old, and some corners are no longer perfect. But that is also why they feel different from new products. They are physical objects with history.

今天买了几张 Michael Jackson 的黑胶唱片，包括《Forever, Michael》《Music & Me》《Off the Wall》，还有一张白标唱片。这还不是一篇很专业的收藏记录，更像是一篇早期学习笔记。最先吸引我的不只是封面上的名字，而是这些东西已经经过时间的感觉。封套有磨损，外面的塑料套也旧了，有些角已经不完美。但也正因为这样，它们和新的商品不一样。它们是带着历史痕迹的实体物品。

I still cannot fully identify every pressing, country, label detail, catalog number, or market value yet. But I am beginning to understand that collecting is not only about liking something. It also means learning how to observe the object itself: the cover, the record label, the release information, the physical condition, and the reason why one version may become more meaningful than another. Future collectibles do not always look important at the beginning. Sometimes they are just old objects sitting in a box, waiting for someone to notice them again.

现在我还不能完全判断每一张的压片版本、发行国家、厂牌细节、编号和市场价值。但我开始明白，收藏不是只看喜欢不喜欢，也要慢慢学会观察物品本身：封面、唱片标签、发行信息、保存状态，以及为什么某一个版本可能比另一个版本更有意义。未来收藏品一开始不一定看起来很重要。有时候它们只是放在箱子里的旧物，等待有人重新注意到它们。

Their value may come from music history, cultural memory, physical media, condition, scarcity, or simply from the fact that they still exist in a world where many things are becoming digital. For now, I will keep these records as part of my early learning journal. Later, when I know more about vinyl records, Michael Jackson, Motown, pressing history, and record preservation, I can come back and update this note.

它们的价值可能来自音乐历史、文化记忆、实体媒介、保存状态、稀缺性，也可能只是因为在很多东西都变成数字的时候，它们仍然真实存在。现在，我先把这几张唱片作为早期学习记录保存下来。以后如果我了解了更多关于黑胶唱片、Michael Jackson、Motown、压片历史和唱片保存的知识，我可以再回来更新这篇记录。

Current observation / 当下观察：

Today I did not only buy several records. I started learning how to look at old objects more carefully.

今天买到的不只是几张唱片，而是开始学习如何更认真地观察旧物。
